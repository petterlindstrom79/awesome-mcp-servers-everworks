## Overview

Ayrshare Social MCP is an enterprise-grade Model Context Protocol server offering coverage across 13+ social media platforms. It provides unified social media management through a single interface, enabling businesses to automate posting, analytics, and engagement across Twitter/X, Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, YouTube, Pinterest, Reddit, Threads, and more.

## Supported Platforms (13+)

- **Twitter/X**: Tweets and engagement
- **Facebook**: Pages and groups
- **Instagram**: Posts, stories, and reels
- **LinkedIn**: Personal and company pages
- **TikTok**: Video content
- **YouTube**: Video uploads and community
- **Pinterest**: Pins and boards
- **Reddit**: Subreddit posting
- **Threads**: Meta's text platform
- **Telegram**: Channel management
- **Google Business Profile**: Business updates
- **And more**: Expanding platform support
