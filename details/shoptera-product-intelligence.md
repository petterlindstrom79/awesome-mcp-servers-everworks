## Overview

Shoptera Product Intelligence provides comprehensive product catalog search across Central European e-commerce, covering approximately 8.5 million products from approximately 2,500 e-shops across 7 countries.

## Coverage

- Approximately 2,500 e-shops
- Approximately 8.5 million products
- 7 countries: Czech Republic (CZ), Slovakia (SK), Poland (PL), Hungary (HU), Romania (RO), Germany (DE), Austria (AT)

## Features

- Semantic search across product catalogs
- Keyword-based product matching
- GTIN/EAN product lookup
- REST API access
- MCP server integration for AI agents
- Multi-country product coverage

## Use Cases

- Price comparison and market analysis
- Product availability tracking
- E-commerce competitive intelligence
- Catalog enrichment
