# DragonMCP

MCP server for Greater China local life services including Meituan/Ele.me food delivery, Didi/Meituan ride-hailing, WeChat Pay/Alipay, Amap/Baidu Maps, 12306 high-speed rail, Taobao/JD/Xianyu e-commerce, Hong Kong government e-services, and more. Supports local, cloud, home, and Apple platforms.
