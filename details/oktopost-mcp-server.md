## Overview

Oktopost is a B2B social media management platform that recently launched MCP support. It is specifically designed for B2B marketing teams that need to align social media with their lead generation funnel. Supports Twitter/X, LinkedIn, and Facebook.

## Features

- Create and schedule B2B-focused social content
- Employee advocacy workflows to distribute content through employee networks
- Social listening and monitoring
- Lead attribution — track which social posts drive actual leads
- CRM integration to connect social media management with the lead pipeline

## Setup

Setup time: 5-10 minutes. MCP integration is newer and still maturing.

## Pricing

Enterprise pricing model — not suitable for individual creators.

## Considerations

- B2B-specific features may be overkill for personal accounts
- Newer MCP integration, still maturing
- Enterprise pricing

## Best For

B2B marketing teams that want to connect their social media management to their CRM and lead pipeline.
