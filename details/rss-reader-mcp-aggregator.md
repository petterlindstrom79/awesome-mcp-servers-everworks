## Overview

RSS Reader MCP Aggregator is a Model Context Protocol server designed for RSS feed aggregation and article content extraction. It allows AI assistants to subscribe to RSS feeds, get article lists, and extract the full content of articles from URLs, formatting them as clean Markdown for easy consumption.

## Features

- **Feed Subscription Management**: Subscribe to and manage multiple RSS/Atom feeds
- **Article Listing**: Retrieve lists of articles from subscribed feeds
- **Content Extraction**: Extract full article content from URLs
- **Markdown Formatting**: Convert extracted content to clean, readable Markdown
- **Feed Discovery**: Automatically discover RSS feeds from websites
- **Filtering**: Filter articles by date, keyword, or category
- **Batch Processing**: Process multiple feeds efficiently
- **Update Monitoring**: Track feed updates and new articles

## Use Cases

- Aggregate news from multiple sources for AI analysis
- Extract and summarize articles through natural language queries
- Monitor industry news and trends automatically
- Build custom news digests with AI curation
- Research topics by collecting relevant articles from RSS feeds
- Create automated content pipelines for analysis
